WB Games is a leader in the games industry across all game platforms and mobile devices. From Mortal Kombat, Game of Thrones, and Batman to Harry Potter, and LEGO, our storytelling and unique player experiences reach millions of gamers across the world.

Our diverse and inclusive workforce creates and brings to life the most beloved characters, franchises, heroes, and wizards in the world.

We are fans of what we make and proud of what we do.

Our WB Analytics team supports our studios and publishing teams worldwide, throughout the lifecycle of each game. We work closely with these partners to continuously refine:
- Our Analytics Platform
- Market Research
- Game Analytics
- Publishing Analytics

Our WBA team has openings worldwide, as well as embedded positions at each studio. Many positions have flexible location options.



Self Advancement & Promotion
WBA is very supportive of both personal enrichment, through training and conferences, as well as supporting personal promotion, through sponsored speaking engagements.

Extremely high retention
WBA has a seasoned team, with a very high retention rate year-over-year. Most members of our team have worked on multiple console generations, and have drawn experience across a wide range of industries.

Advanced Analytics in the Cloud
We have a very close relationship with many Big Data organizations, including Looker, AWS, and the Apache Software Foundation.

In partnership with these organizations, we regularly work ahead of our contemporaries on pre-release features that change the advertising and technology landscape.

Variety
Our team supports a deep cut of the games business that it enables us to regularly cycle everyone through new projects. It’s a great way to see a cross-cut of the games business, and further buff up your experience.

Flexible work arrangements
We want to support you, and that starts with providing an environment where you can do your best work. We have a very flexible “work from home” policy, and if life changes for you down the line, we support remote work for many situations.
